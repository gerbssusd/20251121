# Ad Hominem / Straw Man

<!-- Slide 2 -->

---
layout: center
---

# Context — Ad Hominem / Straw Man

Trump calls Democratic veterans "traitors" and accuses them of "seditious behavior … punishable by death."

<!-- Slide 3 -->

---
layout: center
---

# Explanation — Ad Hominem / Straw Man

Instead of addressing their legal argument, he attacks their character (ad hominem) and misrepresents their stance (straw man).
